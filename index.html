About Real Living

Built on Oak Street. Rooted in Kansas City.

Real Living was founded by Laylani Gonzalez with a simple belief: families in Kansas City deserve a real estate team that speaks their language, knows their neighborhoods, and treats every closing like it's family.

Today, from our brick-and-mortar office at 7017 Oak Street, our team has helped hundreds of families buy and sell more than $30 million in Missouri and Kansas real estate — with a special heart for first-time and Hispanic homebuyers navigating the process for the first time.
