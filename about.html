About Us

The team behind the door on Oak Street

Real Living KC is a locally owned, bilingual real estate team — built in Kansas City, for Kansas City families.

Our Founder

Laylani Gonzalez

Laylani founded Real Living with a conviction shaped by her own community: too many hard-working Kansas City families — especially first-generation and Spanish-speaking households — were being left to navigate the biggest purchase of their lives without a guide who truly spoke their language, in every sense.

So she built the brokerage she wished existed. A real office you can walk into at 7017 Oak Street. Agents who explain contracts in English or español at the kitchen table. A team that treats a $90,000 starter home with the same care as a luxury listing — because behind every door is a family.

Under her leadership, the Real Living team has closed more than $30 million in Missouri and Kansas real estate, while growing into a family of companies that can carry a client from first showing to financing to keys — all under one roof.

Why we do it
