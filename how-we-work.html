How We Work

On your behalf — every step

Whether you're buying your first home or selling your tenth, one team handles the whole journey under one roof — agents, lending guidance, transaction coordination.

For Buyers
We find it, negotiate it, close it.

Private showings on homes that fit your budget — including our own inventory before it hits the market. We negotiate the price and repairs on your side of the table, connect financing (bank, ITIN, or owner-finance paths), and our transaction team walks you to keys in hand.

For Sellers
Marketed like it matters — or sold as-is.

Professional listing with real marketing muscle, priced from live comps. Need speed instead? Our investment arm can make a direct as-is offer — no repairs, no showings. You pick the path; we execute it.

Financing, Explained

Every path to owning a home

Most companies know one road. Because our family of companies includes lending and investment arms, we can walk you down whichever road actually fits your life.

Path 01
Traditional bank loan

Conventional and FHA mortgages, typically 3-5%+ down with established credit and income history. We connect you with trusted local lenders and stay in the deal through underwriting so nothing stalls.

Path 02
ITIN loan - no Social Security required

A real mortgage using your ITIN number: proof of income (including self-employment), a larger down payment, and you own the home in your name. Our bilingual team handles the whole process in Spanish if you prefer.

Path 03
Owner financing

On select homes we own, you can buy directly from us: flexible qualifying based on your down payment and reliable income, monthly payments to us instead of a bank, and real recorded ownership. All credit situations welcome.

Path 04
Investor capital

Buying to renovate or rent? Our lending arm funds investors asset-based: purchase plus rehab on draws, or long-term rental loans qualified on the property's rent. Ask about ASAP Lending.

Real Scenarios

Three families, three roads, three closings

The First Home

A young couple, self-employed, cash income, no Social Security number. Two banks said no. We qualified them on an ITIN path, negotiated the price down, and handled the entire closing in Spanish. Keys in about six weeks.

The Inherited House

A family out of state inherits a home that needs work. No time for repairs, showings, or months on market. Our investment side made a fair as-is offer, we closed in days, and they never flew in once.

The First Flip

An investor finds a distressed house with real margin. Our lending arm funded the purchase and the renovation on draws; when the work was done, our agents listed the finished home. One roof, the entire cycle.
